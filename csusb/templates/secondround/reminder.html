Round Two: Expedition Through the Rainforest

You and your friends have decided to take an expedition through the rainforest in Costa Rica.

Scoring is comprised of four components:

Originality: We compare each of your responses to the responses from a group of 50 students at CSUSB. Responses that were given by only 5% of the students are unusual (1 point), responses that were given by only 1% of the students are unique (2 points).

Fluency: We add up the all the number of responses you gave for each item.

Flexibility: We count the number of different categories your answers fall into ("weapon" and "hit sister" are from the same general idea of weapon).

Elaboration: We look for the amount of detail you gave (for example: "a doorstop" = 0 points, whereas "a doorstop to prevent a door slamming shut in a strong wind" = 2 points-one for the explanation of door slamming, two for further detail about the wind).
